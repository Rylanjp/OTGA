Where It All Started

OTGA began as an idea between family, who had a passion for bonding time in the form of escape rooms. We are a family owned business, started and built entirely by us.

Building the Vision

We grew from late-night planning sessions into something real. Slowly building out and realizing our vision. Eventually seeing it fully come to life.

Bringing It to Life

From concept to reality, OTGA became a place where not only we enjoy being, but that we can bring to other people for them to experience for themselves.
